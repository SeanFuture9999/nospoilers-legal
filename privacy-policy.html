1. Information We Collect

When you use the Services, the following information may be collected automatically:

  • Device information (device model, operating system, language, screen resolution)

  • Application usage data (sessions, feature interactions, in-app events, gameplay progress)

1.2 Information You Provide

The Services do not require account registration. You may provide information voluntarily by:

  • Contacting us by email for support, feedback, or account deletion requests

1.3 Local-Only Game Data

Your gameplay progress (cultivation realm, inventory, skill library, pet collection, settings, etc.) is stored only on your device. We do not upload or retain a copy on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Services
  • To analyze gameplay patterns, balance the game, and prioritize future updates
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs
  • To deliver and measure the performance of rewarded advertisements
  • To process in-app purchases and honor entitlements

4. Advertising and Advertising Identifiers

The Services display rewarded video advertisements served by Google AdMob. Rewarded ads are presented only when you opt in to them in exchange for specific in-game benefits, including:

  • Character revival after death
  • Daily free stamina and spirit stones
  • Pet hatching acceleration
  • Offline/idle reward claims

AdMob may use your Google Advertising ID to deliver relevant advertisements. You may reset or limit ad tracking at any time via:

Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID (or Opt out of Ads Personalization).

5. In-App Purchases

The Services offer a single voluntary in-app purchase: a Supporter Pack. All payments are processed by Google Play Billing. We receive a purchase confirmation token so that we can grant you the corresponding in-game benefits, but we do not collect or store your credit card number, bank account, or other payment credentials.

6. Age Suitability

The Services are rated for all ages (general audience). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the information below and we will take steps to delete it.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your gameplay data is stored locally on your device, deletion can be performed at any time, directly by you:

  • In-game: Settings → Delete Account / Reset Progress. This action erases local data immediately and is irreversible.
  • Device-level: Uninstalling the app removes all local data.
  • Analytics data retained by Firebase/AdMob is governed by Google's retention policies; you may also request deletion by emailing us (see Contact below).

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information handled through the Services. Because the Services do not require user accounts and store gameplay data on-device, the attack surface for personal data exposure is intentionally minimized.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR in the EEA, CCPA in California, PDPA in Taiwan), you may have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent previously given

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the email below.

10. International Data Transfers

Our third-party service providers may process data in countries other than your own (primarily the United States). By using the Services, you consent to such transfers in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through the Services where appropriate.
