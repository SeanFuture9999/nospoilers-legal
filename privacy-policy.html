NoSpoilers ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile applications and services (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage data (app features used, session duration, in-app actions)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address and general location (country or city level)
  • App crash logs, error reports, and performance data

1.2 Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • In-app purchase records
  • Customer support inquiries
  • Feedback and survey responses

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Services
  • To process in-app purchases and transactions
  • To display advertisements (including personalized ads)
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our Services
  • To identify and fix crashes and technical issues
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To respond to customer support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

Our Services use the following third-party services that may collect information:

3.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data to show personalized ads based on your interests. For more information, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

3.2 Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our Services. Firebase collects usage data and device information to help us improve the user experience. For more information, please visit: Firebase Privacy

3.3 Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and error logs. This helps us identify and fix technical issues to improve app stability. Data collected includes:

  • Crash stack traces and error messages
  • Device model, OS version, and device orientation
  • App state at the time of crash
  • Crashlytics installation UUID (anonymous identifier)

This data is used solely for improving app stability and does not include personally identifiable information.

3.4 Cloud Firestore

We use Google Cloud Firestore to store and sync limited app data, such as redemption code validation. This service may process:

  • Anonymized user identifiers
  • Redemption code usage records

No personal information (such as name, email, or phone number) is stored in Firestore.

3.5 In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through the respective app store (Google Play Store or Apple App Store). We do not directly collect or store your payment information. Please refer to the privacy policies of the respective platforms.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our Services
  • With advertising partners (in aggregated or anonymized form)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. When information is no longer needed, we will delete or anonymize it.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Opt-out of personalized ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings or using the opt-out mechanisms provided by ad networks.
  • Access and deletion: You may request access to or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.
  • Data portability: Where applicable, you may request a copy of your data in a portable format.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

隱私權政策

NoSpoilers（以下簡稱「我們」）尊重您的隱私。本隱私權政策說明當您使用我們的行動應用程式及服務（統稱「服務」）時，我們如何收集、使用、揭露及保護您的資訊。

使用我們的服務即表示您同意依照本政策收集和使用資訊。

1. 我們收集的資訊

1.1 自動收集的資訊

當您使用我們的服務時，我們可能自動收集以下資訊：

  • 裝置資訊（裝置型號、作業系統版本、唯一裝置識別碼）
  • 使用資料（使用的應用程式功能、工作階段時間、應用程式內操作）
  • 廣告識別碼（如 Google 廣告 ID）
  • IP 位址及一般位置（國家或城市層級）
  • 應用程式當機記錄、錯誤報告及效能資料

1.2 您提供的資訊

我們可能收集您自願提供的資訊，例如：

  • 應用程式內購買記錄
  • 客戶支援查詢
  • 意見回饋及問卷調查回覆

2. 資訊使用方式

我們將收集的資訊用於以下目的：

  • 提供及維護我們的服務
  • 處理應用程式內購買及交易
  • 顯示廣告（包括個人化廣告）
  • 分析使用模式並改善我們的服務
  • 識別並修復當機及技術問題
  • 偵測及防止詐欺或濫用行為
  • 回應客戶支援請求
  • 遵守法律義務

3. 第三方服務

我們的服務使用以下可能收集資訊的第三方服務：

3.1 Google AdMob

我們使用 Google AdMob 顯示廣告。AdMob 可能收集並處理資料，以根據您的興趣顯示個人化廣告。如需更多資訊，請造訪：Google 隱私權政策

3.2 Firebase Analytics

我們使用 Firebase Analytics 了解使用者如何與我們的服務互動。Firebase 會收集使用資料及裝置資訊，幫助我們改善使用者體驗。如需更多資訊，請造訪：Firebase 隱私權

3.3 Firebase Crashlytics

我們使用 Firebase Crashlytics 收集當機報告及錯誤記錄，幫助我們識別並修復技術問題以提升應用程式穩定性。收集的資料包括：

  • 當機堆疊追蹤及錯誤訊息
  • 裝置型號、作業系統版本及裝置方向
  • 當機時的應用程式狀態
  • Crashlytics 安裝 UUID（匿名識別碼）

這些資料僅用於改善應用程式穩定性，不包含可識別個人身份的資訊。

3.4 Cloud Firestore

我們使用 Google Cloud Firestore 儲存及同步有限的應用程式資料，例如兌換碼驗證。此服務可能處理：

  • 匿名化的使用者識別碼
  • 兌換碼使用記錄

Firestore 中不會儲存任何個人資訊（如姓名、電子郵件或電話號碼）。

3.5 應用程式內購買

應用程式內購買透過各應用程式商店（Google Play 商店或 Apple App Store）處理。我們不會直接收集或儲存您的付款資訊。請參閱各平台的隱私權政策。

我們不會出售您的個人資訊。我們可能在以下情況下分享您的資訊：

  • 與協助營運我們服務的服務供應商分享
  • 與廣告合作夥伴分享（以彙總或匿名形式）
